About Us

Lumé Bracelet is a bracelet brand that brings beauty in simplicity. Each bracelet is designed with a gentle touch, neat details, and soothing color nuances to complement your daily style.

We believe that accessories are not just complements, but a way to express yourself. Therefore, each Lumé bracelet is made with care to feel comfortable to wear, easy to mix and match, and still look elegant in every moment.

With a combination of minimalist design and pastel colors such as light purple, light blue, and pink, Lumé Bracelet is suitable for those of you who like a soft, feminine, and timeless look.
